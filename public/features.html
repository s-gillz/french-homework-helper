Powerful Features for French Success

Everything your child needs to excel in French Immersion, powered by advanced AI.

📸

Homework Helper

Upload a photo of any French homework. The AI translates it, explains the grammar rules, and gives parents step-by-step coaching instructions.

📖

Daily Reading

Grade-appropriate French passages with audio narration and comprehension questions to build reading skills.

📝

Interactive Quizzes

Daily 5-question quizzes that adapt to your child's grade level, covering grammar, vocabulary, and comprehension.

🎙️

Pronunciation Practice

Record your voice and get a score from 0-100 with specific tips on how to improve French phonetics and accent.

✍️

La Dictée

The ultimate French spelling test. Listen to AI-read passages and type what you hear. Get detailed error corrections.

💬

Conversation Chat

Practice real French conversations with "Madame AI", who gently corrects mistakes and keeps the chat going.

🎯

Targeted Practice

The AI identifies weak areas (like plural nouns or passé composé) and generates custom quizzes to fix them.

🧠

Smart Flashcards

Spaced repetition system ensures vocabulary learned today is remembered forever.

📊

Progress Dashboard

Track XP, streaks, quiz scores, and weak areas. Parents get weekly email reports on their child's progress.
