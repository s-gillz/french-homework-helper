Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Simply click "Start Free Trial" and create an account. No credit card required. You can start using the app immediately!

Is this aligned with Ontario curriculum?

Yes! All our content is specifically designed for Ontario French Immersion Grades 1-8, aligned with Ministry of Education expectations.

Can I use this on my phone or tablet?

Absolutely! French Learning Hub works on all devices - desktop, laptop, tablet, and smartphone.

What if I have multiple children?

Our Family plan ($49.99/month) supports up to 3 student profiles, and our Ultimate Family plan ($69.99/month) supports up to 5 students.

I'm a teacher. Do you offer school accounts?

Yes! We offer free and discounted tiers for French Immersion teachers. Contact us at hello@thestudyzone.ca to learn more about classroom licensing.
