About French Learning Hub

Empowering French Immersion families across Ontario with innovative AI-powered learning tools.

Our Story

French Learning Hub was created by The Study Zone to address a critical challenge faced by thousands of families across Brampton, Mississauga, Caledon, and the Greater Toronto Area: supporting children in French Immersion when parents don't speak French themselves.

We saw parents struggling to help with homework, students losing confidence in their pronunciation, and families paying $50-80/hour for private tutoring that wasn't always available when needed most.

Our solution? Combine cutting-edge artificial intelligence with proven pedagogical methods to create an affordable, always-available, curriculum-aligned French tutor that works for the way families live today.

Our Mission

"To make high-quality French Immersion support accessible to every Ontario family, regardless of their budget, schedule, or own French proficiency."
